## <a name="introduction"></a> Introduction
<p>
    Human resources (HR) management has been an important aspect of organizational management for many years as it takes care of 
    the organization's most valuabe asset; it's employees. Which is why the main focus of HR professionals has always been to assure 
    their employees everything they need to excel in their day-to-day tasks. However with the continous growth of companies and their manpower retaining qualified employees and a healthy work environment got harder.
    The development of HR systems has revolutionized the way businesses approach HR management, as it allows for easy storage of employees information, manage them properly by automating many of the tasks that were previously performed manually and unlocks the benefit of time allowing HR department members to focus on high-impact projects. <br>
    In this document we will discuss HR system in further details, the workflow, the features, the benefits of using it and it's continously growing market.

</p>
